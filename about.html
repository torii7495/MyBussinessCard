My Story

Why I Started Tori's Tech Home Help

Technology has always been my thing. Not just using it — understanding it. Taking it apart with my mind, figuring out how it works, and then turning around and explaining it to someone else in plain language. That's the part I love most.

But here's what I kept seeing: the world's technology is moving at an incredible pace, and a lot of people — good, smart, capable people — are being left behind. Not because they can't learn. Because nobody has taken the time to teach them with patience and without making them feel foolish for asking.

"Technology should connect people to the world — not make them feel shut out of it. That's exactly what drove me to start this business."

I started Tori's Tech Home Help because I believe everyone deserves to feel confident with the devices in their own home. Whether you're 45 or 85, whether you've had a smartphone for ten years or just got your first one — if you need help, you deserve someone who will sit down with you, take their time, and not leave until you actually understand.

I'm based right here in Murfreesboro, TN — and this community is mine. I'm not a call center, not a big-box store, not an app. I'm a real person who genuinely loves technology and genuinely loves helping people. That combination is rare, and it's the foundation of everything I do.

What I Stand For

My Core Values

These aren't just words on a page — they're the standard I hold myself to in every single session.

01

Honesty Above Everything

If a problem is outside my ability, I will always tell you upfront — clearly and kindly — and do my best to point you toward someone who can help. You will never pay for a problem I couldn't solve. That's a promise.

02

Patience Without Limit

Technology can be frustrating, and feeling confused is never your fault. I take all the time you need — no rushing, no eye rolls, no making you feel bad for asking the same question twice. You'll leave every session feeling more confident than when we started.

03

Teaching, Not Just Fixing

Anyone can come in, poke around, and walk out. I explain what I'm doing and why, so that next time you'll know too. The goal is your independence — not your dependence on me.

04

Dedication to the Finish

I don't give up on problems. If something stumps me, I research it, dig into it, and come back with answers. You hired me to solve a problem — and I won't stop until I do, or I've found someone who can.

05

Respect for Your Privacy

Your devices contain your life. I never access anything without your permission, I never store your personal information, and I treat everything I see with complete discretion.

06

Fair, Transparent Pricing

You'll always know what a session costs before we start. No surprise charges, no upselling, no confusion. Just honest work at a fair price — every single time.
